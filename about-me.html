Values As A Developer

As a developing programmer, I place a strong emphasis on clean, organized code that remains readable and maintainable over time. I prioritize minimalistic, user-focused design because I believe that thoughtful visuals and simplicity are essential to a strong user experience. Although I use AI as a supportive tool, my goal is always to understand the logic behind the code I create and continue building my skills independently. I aim to produce projects that are practical, purposeful, and enjoyable to use, rather than overly complex for the sake of complexity. Quality, usability, and visual refinement guide my development process, and I consistently hold myself to delivering work that reflects those values.
